Team

Karasuno

Alright, I already know I've upset quite a lot of people from this choice. While Karasuno is the main team and is expected to be everyone's number one team, they aren't for me. I highly admire this team and each one of its members. The only problem that I do have, is the lack of development from certain characters. Ennoshita did have his time to shine and showed how perfectly he could be a future captain. Narita didn't even have ten minutes to take a stand for his character. Kinoshita hasn't even been on the court at all. The underrated second years is what keeps Karasuno a bit lower on my list.

Nekoma

What can I say about this team without rambling too much? Not only are they one of the top teams, they also have a very well-rounded team. Not shown much in the anime, each team member gets their time to shine in the manga. Individual character development does take place in the manga revolving around Lev and Shibayama. Kenma is even shown opening up to Hinata when they first meet. The way that Kuroo connects his team together with that (cheesy) pre-game phrase really shows you what this team is made of.

Fukurodani

Hey hey hey! It's the owls at number four! Along with Shiratorizawa, not much is seen from this team in the anime. Their true potential just skims the surface of what they can really achieve in the anime. One thing that did throw me off with this team is it only focuses on Bokuto and Akaashi. Konoha does get time to shine in the manga, but only for one chapter. The way the team functions is mostly around Bokuto having his random mood swings. Of course, the team is always there to cheer the poor guy up
